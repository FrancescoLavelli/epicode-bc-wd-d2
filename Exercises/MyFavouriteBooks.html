My Favourite Books

TOP 5 Books

March 22, 2025

My passion for reading began at a school desk, sparked by the timeless adventure of *Ivanhoe* by Sir Walter Scott. As I turned the pages of this classic tale of knights, chivalry, and daring quests, I was transported to a world far beyond the confines of the classroom. The vivid characters, rich historical setting, and thrilling plot captivated my imagination, making me realize the power of books to open doors to new worlds and perspectives. That first encounter with *Ivanhoe* ignited a lifelong love for reading, as I discovered the joy of losing myself in stories, exploring different eras, and connecting with the emotions and ideas of characters. From that moment on, books became my companions, teachers, and endless sources of inspiration.

  1. The Lord of the Rings
  2. Ivanhoe
  3. The Killing Chronicles
  4. American Gods
  5. 1984
